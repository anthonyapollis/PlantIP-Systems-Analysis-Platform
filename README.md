# Plant Variety IP & Royalty Licensing Management Platform

Business and systems analysis portfolio case study: a business process review, legacy Microsoft Access systems audit, and PostgreSQL migration design for an organisation managing plant breeders' rights, licensing, and royalty collection.

**Live case study:** https://anthonyapollis.github.io/PlantIP-Systems-Analysis-Platform/

> This is a synthetic, independently-built recreation for portfolio purposes. All organisation names, growers, cultivars, and figures are fictional. It is not derived from, and does not reproduce, any real employer's or client's data or systems.

## What this demonstrates

- **Business process mapping** across the plant variety IP lifecycle (importation, quarantine, registration, propagation, licensing, royalty collection)
- **Current-state systems audit** of a legacy Microsoft Access database against business requirements
- **Requirements elicitation** consolidated into a normalised target data model
- **Entity-relationship modelling** across 8 subject areas / 10 tables
- **PostgreSQL migration design**, including DDL, indexing strategy, and phased migration approach
- **Formal requirements documentation**: Business Requirements Document, requirements traceability matrix, and as-is/to-be business process diagrams
- **Stakeholder presentation** for steering committee sign-off
