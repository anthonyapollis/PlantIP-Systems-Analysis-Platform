Plant Variety IP & Royalty
Licensing Management Platform

Systems audit findings and recommended PostgreSQL migration.

Prepared by Anthony Apollis, Business & Systems Analyst

1 · The problem

The current system can't keep up

  • Royalty rates and payment status live in disconnected Access tables and spreadsheets.
  • Reconciliation is manual, re-run every cycle, with no audit trail when discrepancies occur.
  • Single-file Access architecture blocks concurrent multi-user access.
  • Quarantine and pathology results are not consistently linked back to the blocks they govern.

The data itself is well understood by the business — it's the system that can't enforce or scale the rules the business already follows.

4 · Recommendation

Migrate to PostgreSQL, phased by risk

  • Phase 1 — Licensing & royalty (highest business risk)
  • Phase 2 — Block, pathology & quarantine
  • Phase 3 — Nursery orders
  • Enforced referential integrity across every subject area from day one of each phase.
